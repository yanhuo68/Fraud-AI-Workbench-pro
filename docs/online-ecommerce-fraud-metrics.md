# Online / E-commerce Fraud Detection Metrics

Applies to:
- Payment fraud
- Account takeover
- Promotion abuse
- Refund fraud

---

## 1. Payment Fraud Metrics

### Payment Fraud Rate
Fraudulent Orders / Total Orders

### Chargeback Ratio
Chargebacks / Total Transactions

### Fraud Loss Rate
Fraud Loss / Gross Merchandise Value (GMV)

---

## 2. Customer Experience Metrics

### False Decline Rate
Legitimate Orders Declined / Legitimate Orders

### Approval Rate
Approved Orders / Total Orders

### Customer Abandonment Rate
Checkout Drop-off After Fraud Challenge
