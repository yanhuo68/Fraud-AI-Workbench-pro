# Residual Fraud Risk Measurement

Residual Risk = Inherent Risk – Control Effectiveness

Or

Residual Risk = Inherent Risk × (1 – Control Strength)

---

## Residual Risk Scoring Matrix

| Inherent Risk | Control Strength | Residual Risk |
|---------------|------------------|---------------|
| High | Strong | Medium |
| High | Weak | High |
| Medium | Strong | Low |

---

## Residual Risk Classification

- Low
- Moderate
- High
- Critical

---

## Escalation Triggers

- Residual Risk = High or Critical
- Control Failure Identified
- Emerging Fraud Pattern Unmitigated
