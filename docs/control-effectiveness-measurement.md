# Fraud Control Effectiveness Measurement

Control Effectiveness measures how well fraud controls reduce risk.

---

## Control Types

- Preventive controls
- Detective controls
- Corrective controls

---

## Measurement Methods

### 1. Control Design Effectiveness
Is the control properly designed?

Scored:
- Effective
- Partially Effective
- Ineffective

---

### 2. Control Operating Effectiveness
Does the control work consistently?

Measured by:
- Testing pass rate
- Exception rate
- Audit findings
- Model performance stability

---

## Control Effectiveness Score Example

Design Score (1–5)
Operating Score (1–5)

Weighted Score = (Design × 40%) + (Operating × 60%)
