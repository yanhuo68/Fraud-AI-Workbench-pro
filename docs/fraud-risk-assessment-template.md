# 3. Inherent Risk Assessment

## 3.1 Risk Scoring Methodology

Likelihood Scale (1–5):
1 = Rare
5 = Almost Certain

Impact Scale (1–5):
1 = Minimal financial or reputational impact
5 = Severe financial, regulatory, or reputational damage

Inherent Risk Score = Likelihood × Impact
