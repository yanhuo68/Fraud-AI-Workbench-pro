# Fraud Risk Measurement Framework

Fraud Risk Measurement focuses on evaluating exposure, control strength, and residual risk.

---

## Risk Formula

Fraud Risk = Likelihood × Impact

Residual Risk = Inherent Risk – Control Effectiveness

---

## Core Components

1. Inherent Risk
2. Control Effectiveness
3. Residual Risk
4. Key Risk Indicators (KRIs)
5. Risk Appetite Monitoring
6. Escalation & Governance

---

## Three Lines of Defense Mapping

1LoD: Owns fraud risk in business processes  
2LoD: Defines risk methodology & oversight  
3LoD: Independent assurance
