# Fraud Risk Appetite & Escalation Framework

---

## Risk Appetite Statement Example

The organization maintains a low tolerance for fraud loss exceeding 0.05% of annual revenue.

---

## Quantitative Risk Appetite Metrics

- Fraud Loss Rate <= X%
- Residual Risk Score <= Medium
- False Positive Rate <= Y%
- Investigation Backlog <= Z days

---

## Escalation Thresholds

| Breach Type | Escalation Level |
|-------------|------------------|
| Risk Appetite Breach | CRO Notification |
| Control Failure | Immediate Remediation Plan |
| Regulatory Finding | Board Risk Committee |

---

## Reporting Frequency

- Monthly: Executive Committee
- Quarterly: Board Risk Committee
- Annually: Enterprise Risk Review
