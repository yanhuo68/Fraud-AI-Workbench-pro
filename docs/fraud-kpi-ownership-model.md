# Fraud Detection KPI Ownership Model
(Three Lines of Defense Framework)

This document defines KPI ownership under the Three Lines of Defense (3LoD) governance model.

---

# 1. Governance Structure Overview

## First Line of Defense (1LoD) – Business / Operations
- Fraud Operations
- Risk Operations
- Product Teams
- Business Owners

Primary responsibility:
- Own fraud risk
- Execute controls
- Monitor daily KPIs
- Take corrective action

---

## Second Line of Defense (2LoD) – Risk & Compliance
- Enterprise Risk
- Model Risk Management
- Compliance
- Financial Crime Risk

Primary responsibility:
- Set policies & standards
- Independent challenge
- Risk appetite monitoring
- Oversight & validation

---

## Third Line of Defense (3LoD) – Internal Audit

Primary responsibility:
- Independent assurance
- Governance effectiveness review
- Control testing
- Regulatory readiness assessment
