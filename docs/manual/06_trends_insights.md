# 06. Trends & Insights

The **Analytics Hub** provides a macro-level view of fraud activity. Instead of focusing on individual transactions, this module identifies high-level patterns, volume surges, and geographical hotspots.

## 📊 1. Automated Dashboards

- **KPI Tracking**: Real-time monitoring of total fraud volume, average loss per incident, and case resolution time.
- **Trend Visualization**: Interactive Altair charts that show fraud frequency over time, categorized by transaction type.
- **Heatmaps**: Identify geographical regions or IP blocks with disproportionately high risk levels.

## 📈 2. Predictive Insights

The system uses historical data to forecast future fraud trends.
- **Peak Alerting**: AI identifies specific days or times (e.g., holidays) where fraud attempts traditionally spike.
- **Category Shifts**: Detects if fraudsters are moving away from traditional wire transfers toward newer methods like digital wallets or crypto.

## 📝 3. Narrative Reporting

Beyond charts, the **Sentinel Operator** provides a natural language summary of the current data state.
- *"Fraud volume has increased by 15% in the 'Transfer' category compared to last month."*
- *"We are seeing a high concentration of invalid login attempts from region X."*

> [!TIP]
> Use the **Date Range** filters to refine your analysis to specific investigation windows.
