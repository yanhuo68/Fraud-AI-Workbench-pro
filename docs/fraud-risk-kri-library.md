# Fraud Risk Key Risk Indicators (KRIs)

KRIs measure risk exposure, not model performance.

---

## Exposure KRIs

- Fraud Loss as % of Revenue
- Fraud Loss Growth Rate
- High-Risk Customer Ratio
- New Fraud Typology Emergence Rate

---

## Control Weakness KRIs

- Model Drift Index (PSI > 0.25)
- Control Failure Rate
- Audit Findings Count
- Policy Exception Rate

---

## Operational Risk KRIs

- Alert Backlog Ratio
- Investigator Overcapacity Ratio
- SLA Breach Frequency

---

## External Risk KRIs

- Industry Fraud Trend Increase
- Regulatory Enforcement Actions
- Dark Web Threat Indicators
