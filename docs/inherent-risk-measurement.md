# Inherent Fraud Risk Measurement

Inherent Risk = Risk exposure before controls.

---

## Measurement Dimensions

### 1. Product Risk
- Credit card
- Wire transfer
- Online onboarding
- Insurance claims

### 2. Channel Risk
- Digital
- Branch
- Agent/Broker
- Third-party API

### 3. Geographic Risk
- High-risk jurisdictions
- Sanctions exposure

### 4. Customer Risk
- High-risk industry
- Politically exposed persons
- Complex ownership structures

---

## Inherent Risk Scoring Example

Likelihood Score (1–5)
Impact Score (1–5)

Inherent Risk Score = Likelihood × Impact

Example:
Likelihood = 4  
Impact = 5  
Score = 20 (High Risk)

---

## Data Inputs

- Historical fraud rate
- Fraud typology exposure
- Industry benchmarking
- External threat intelligence
