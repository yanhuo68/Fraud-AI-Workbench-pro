## 1. Claims Fraud Metrics

### Fraudulent Claims Rate
Fraudulent Claims / Total Claims

### Fraud Savings

### Average Fraud Claim Value

### Claims Referral Rate

## 2. Special Investigation Unit (SIU) Metrics

### SIU Hit Rate
Confirmed Fraud / Investigated Cases

### Investigation Cycle Time
Average Days to Close Fraud Case

### Recovery Rate
Recovered Amount / Fraud Amount
